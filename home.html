A technical community for college students

TechShala is a technical community for college students, providing opportunities for students to showcase their technical skills and learn from each other. We organize various technical events, workshops, and seminars on different topics such as coding, web development, app development, and more. Tech Shala is an initiative by Vidyalankar, an educational institution based in Mumbai, India, that provides training and education in the field of technology. Tech Shala offers various courses and training programs in areas such as data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and full-stack development.
